      Unframed Art Prints

      Dispatched within 2 - 5 working days

      Printed on a beautiful heavyweight matte art paper using vibrant archival inks for a quality print finish. Carefully wrapped and shipped in heavy mailing tubes for safe arrival.

      Available in standard sizes to fit off the shelf frames, custom sizes are available on request. We love the IKEA frame range as these offer great value. Also worth a mention are Spotlight, Kmart and Country Road along with many other stores offering frames that our prints fit into perfectly.

      Listed sizes refer to the paper size, the optional white border is internal and will not alter the size. If you prefer having ink all the way to the edge, then select ‘no’ white border.

      Framed Prints

      Dispatched within 5-10 working days

      We pride ourselves on quality, our framed prints are professional made by local craftsmen using sustainably sourced solid timber and quality materials.

      We use a contemporary timber moulding available in natural oak, black or white finish. Mouldings have a face width of 20mm and depth of 40mm. 90 x 120 and 100 x 140 sizes have a 25mm face and 45mm depth which has a serious presence as a large artwork.

      Protected behind glass or shatterproof plexiglass for larger sizes. Framed prints are finished and sealed with backing tape to keep dust out, hanging cord and felt wall protectors. Securely packaged and safely delivered around Australia, ready to hang.

      Canvas Prints

      Dispatched within 7-10 working days

      All 41 Orchard designs are available on canvas with an optional float frame. See our Canvas Prints page to browse our most popular canvas designs. If the design you are after is not listed, please get in touch to arrange a canvas version.

      Printed on a premium satin canvas using archival inks to produce a vibrant print that is durable and easy to clean. Professionally stretched and gallery wrapped around 32mm timber stretcher bars with artwork mirrored around the edge.

      Add an optional float frame around the edge of your canvas to give further depth and enhance your artwork. Delivered ready to hang with wall protectors.

      Shipping

      All our products are made to order and have different production times.

      Wall Decals : Dispatch in 2-3 working days
      Unframed Prints : Dispatch in 1-5 working days
      Framed Prints : Dispatch in 4-10 working days
      Canvas Prints : Dispatch in 4-10 working days
      Wallpaper : Dispatch in 4-10 working days

      Orders are shipped via Fastway Couriers or Australia Post from our factory in Geelong Victoria. Depending on your location, under normal circumstances your order will arrive within 2-5 working days from the date of dispatch for metro areas and longer for rural.

      You will receive a shipping email with tracking information when your order is dispatched. If you are not home at the time of delivery, a card will be left with instructions for re-delivery. Alternatively you can select 'authority to leave' at checkout to have your package left at your door.

      The above times are a guide only and not a guarantee your order will arrive within a set time frame. During peak delivery times (Nov- Dec) or due to unforeseen circumstances there may be delay’s with shipping providers. If you need your order by a certain date please get in touch to discuss the options.

      Returns

      Change of mind
      All 41 Orchard products are custom made to order according to the design, size and framing options you choose; we are unable to return these items back into stock as it’s unlikely another customer will choose the same options as you. Therefore, we do not accept returns based on change of mind. Please choose carefully and make sure you are happy with your choice before purchasing.

      Lower value items such as small posters and decals are also made to order but can be returned for store credit but not for cash refunds. Shipping costs are non-refundable and return postage is the responsibility of the customer.

      Damaged in transit
      When shipping large fragile items there is the possibility of damage, thankfully due to considerable protective packaging this is a very rare occurrence for us.

      If items are damaged during shipping please contact us immediately via email within 24 hours of receiving the product and provide photo’s of the damage along with packaging. We will then supply a replacement at no cost to you.

      We do not refund damaged items, a replacement will be offered. A return postage label will be provided to return the damaged item back to us.
